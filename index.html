Our Story

Built in the Region.
Built for the Planet.

Flahtik emerged from a simple observation: the most consequential decisions about land, water, and food are still being made without reliable spatial data. Governments were guessing. Farmers were reacting. Utilities were failing to anticipate.

We set out to change that — combining satellite earth observation, ground-sensor networks, and machine learning into a single intelligence platform purpose-built for the environmental challenges of the 21st century.

Today, Flahtik operates across three continents, partnering with governments, agribusinesses, and water utilities to turn planetary signals into decisions that matter.
